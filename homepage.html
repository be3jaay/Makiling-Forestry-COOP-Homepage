Makiling Forestry Credit Cooperative (MFCC) is a renowned financial institution dedicated to empowering local communities and promoting sustainable forestry practices. Services We Offer

MFCC provides a variety of financial services such as loans, deposits, and online loans. Whether you need financial assistance, want to expand your savings, or want a quick online loan solution, MFCC is here to help. About Makiling Forestry COOP

We are a business credit cooperative that specializes in providing affordable and accessible credit solutions to small and medium-sized enterprises. Our mission is to support the growth and success of our members by offering personalized financial services and fostering a collaborative community environment.

Know more

Welcome to Makiling Forestry Credit Cooperative, a leading and trusted business credit cooperative dedicated to empowering small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). At Makiling Forestry Credit Cooperative, we understand the unique challenges faced by entrepreneurs and business owners in accessing affordable credit and financial resources. Our cooperative is built on the principles of collaboration, transparency, and mutual support, with a mission to provide our members with access to reliable credit solutions and essential financial tools. We believe that by pooling our resources and knowledge, we can create a thriving ecosystem where businesses can flourish and achieve their full potential.

Know more

As a member of Makiling Forestry Credit Cooperative, you gain access to a wide range of benefits and services tailored to meet your business needs. Our cooperative offers flexible and competitive credit products, such as business loans, lines of credit, and trade finance solutions, designed to support your growth and expansion plans. We understand that each business is unique, which is why we take a personalized approach to credit assessment, considering not only traditional financial metrics but also the potential of your business and its future prospects. In addition to credit services, we provide educational resources, networking opportunities, and expert advice to help you navigate the challenges of running a successful business.

What sets Makiling Forestry Credit Cooperative apart is our commitment to building a strong and supportive community of like-minded business owners. We believe in the power of collaboration and aim to foster connections among our members, allowing you to tap into a network of experienced entrepreneurs, industry experts, and potential business partners. By joining Makiling Forestry Credit Cooperative, you become part of a larger collective working together to overcome financial hurdles, share knowledge, and create opportunities for sustainable growth. We take pride in our member-centric approach, focusing on your success and actively seeking ways to provide innovative solutions that address your evolving needs. Discover the difference of being part of Makiling Forestry Credit Cooperative and join us on the path to business success.
